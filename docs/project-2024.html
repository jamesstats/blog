3 Project 2024

Looking at the 2024 presidential election polling between President Biden and Trump in what would be a re-match of 2020.

3.1 Polling with 3rd Parties

Third party candidates keep Biden and Trump below or just above 40’s in their re-match according to these polls.

Figure 3.1: How Third Parties candidates would affect the election

3.1.1 Under 35 Age Group

Robert F.Kennedy Jr has strong support from the under 35 who seems to move away from both Biden and Trump and keep him in the low to high teens.

3.2 Black voters

Biden is held in the 60’s with black voters who he won with 90% of the vote in 2020 with Trump hoovering between 15% and 20% more than the 10% he received in 2020.

3.3 Hispanic voters

Robert F.Kennedy Jr seems to be taking hispanic voters from both Biden and Trump as he hoovers around 20% while keeping them below 40%.
